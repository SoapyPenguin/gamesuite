How to play Imposter

You have a new identity: either you are now a different person in the room or you're the randomly chosen imposter!


If you're not the Imposter:

You have a new identity. Try to act as close as possible to the person whose name is on your screen. You also have a special scenario or condition that complicates the matter, but remember: you should react in the same way as your person would. In other words, don't be yourself. Your goal is to find the person in the room who doesn't have a name printed on their screen before the time runs out. Feel free to interrogate, but if you're not clever enough, the Imposter will catch on and find out who isn't being impersonated. If they do that and thoroughly convince you until the time runs out, the Imposter wins.


If you're the Imposter:

Blend in! You may not have an identity printed on your screen, but there is someone in the room who isn't being impersonated by someone else, and your job is to find out who that is, impersonate them, and convince your friends with sound alibis that you are not the Imposter. Do this until time runs out and you win.
